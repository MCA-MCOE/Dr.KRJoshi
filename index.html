About


Honored

Dr. Mrs. K.R.Joshi is the Principal of P.E.S. Modern College of Engineering, Pune since 2008. She was felicitated by IEEE Communication Society and Institution of Engineers(India), Pune on Occasion of World Telecommunication Day.

Accomplishments

She has more than 33 years of teaching experience. She is an author of the book "Image and Video Compression:Fundamentals, Techniques and Applications, CRC Press, USA" which is been listed in SCOPUS.

Leadership

She was "Session Chair" in International Conference on Information Processing 2015 (ICIP 2015) at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology. She was Chair of, IEEE Signal Processing Society Chapter-Pune Section.

Commitments

She was a Convener for 'Image and Signal Processing Project Competition' organized by IEEE SPS Chapter, Pune Section and IEEE-WIE Affinity Group, Pune Section consecutively for three years (2009-2011).
